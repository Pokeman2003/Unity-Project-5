Controls!
    Player
  • W/S-Move forward/backward!
  • A/D-Turn left/right!
  • Space-Jump!
  • Mouse 1-Fire Rocket!
  • Z-Toggle jetpack!
    Some extra things to note:
  • You do not have aerial control unless you have jetboots[UNOBTAINABLE IN THIS BUILD] or you activate your jetpack.
  • Health regen begins after 3 seconds, and recharges 3 units every second.
  • You reload a rocket every 150 milliseconds.
  • Fuel regen begins after 800 milliseconds, and recharges .5 units every second(or 1 unit every second if you have increased fuel.)
